Hi, I'm Sherri Fisher!

I see in you what will unleash your potential. Things that we believe about ourselves and the world are very powerful. Your current challenges are not a marker of your future ability to succeed. With me as your learning advocate and your ally, together we will unleash your most epic self.

I began my professional career working with bright adolescents who struggled with learning, attention, and organization. When these students learned to magnify and leverage their strengths and identify their success patterns, they flourished in spite of their challenges. My clients have gone on to attend elite colleges, launch successful companies, and build flourishing relationships, careers, and businesses. I am the world’s first learning specialist to earn the University of Pennsylvania’s Master of Applied Positive Psychology (MAPP) degree, and I have the privilege to learn and train with the top researchers and practitioners in the field. My model for improving well-being, the POS-EDGE™®., is inspired by Martin Seligman’s PERMA+ theory of well-being.

Unleash Your Epic Self: The Guide to Crushing it at School, Work and Life

This book incorporates the most powerful personal change research and activities from the exciting science of Positive Psychology and makes them easy to understand and implement. The dynamic 36-week book-journal-workbook includes engaging activities in a full-color experience with edgy graphics. It’s an excellent read, a place to learn and reflect, and a brilliant resource for parents, teens, and education professionals to grow their well-being skills by living them daily. Gain a deeper understanding of what gives you a sense of satisfaction and joy. Imagine and create your best future. Harness your strengths, manage procrastination and strengthen people connections. Rig your everyday decisions for greatness. Every activity is student and educator tested!

Sherri Fisher MAPP, MEd has turned her work with at-risk students of all ages into a self-guided journey where the user can create new possibilities for change, action, and innovation. Unleash Your Epic Self teaches you to identify the root causes of success to intentionally construct a positive and more effective future in school, work and life.
