Learning Specialist, Executive Coach, Writer, Speaker

I began my professional career working with bright adolescents who struggled with learning, attention, and organization. When these students learned to magnify and leverage strengths and identify success patterns, they flourished despite challenges. My international private practice empowers students of all ages to make constructive choices about personal behavior, social interactions, academic performance and career fitness. I enjoy life-long relationships with thousands of clients who have gone on to attend elite colleges, enjoy flourishing relationships and families, shape successful careers, and launch thriving businesses.

As a graduate of the first UPenn Master of Applied Positive Psychology (MAPP) class, I am privileged to have learned and trained with the top researchers and practitioners in the field. My model, the POS-EDGE®, is inspired by Martin Seligman's PERMA+ theory of well-being. I am a founding collaborator and executive coach for Maroondah, Victoria, Australia's Communities of Wellbeing project, an initiative at the nexus of education, business, government, and social services. I'm also a writer of books and articles, and speak to a variety of audiences both live and remotely.
